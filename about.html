What?

The 'Thrasher' progressive web app

Why?

Because this application will help you take care of our planet. It will not only help you to idenitify to which container your trash should be trown in but it will allow you to help other people and correct them if they are mistaken. The main goal of Trasher is to show everyone how thrashed should be recycled by choosing correct waste container

How?

It's simple. Just scan the barcode of a trash, and try to find it in our database. If it not exist, just add as a new trash to help other poeple recognize it in the future.
